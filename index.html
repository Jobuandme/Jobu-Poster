Jobu Poster

Over my winter term reading week, I spent several days creating this poster to strengthen my illustration skills. I began with a rough idea and sketch; it took several attempts to get something I was happy with. In the early stages of creation, I was struggling to get the letters to look how I wanted, but I continued on. Ignoring my thoughts in the early stages of creation allowed me to push on and have an excellent final product come together. Having lost a full day of progress from a Photoshop crash, making this poster really taught me to trust the process as well as that sticking to your vision/plan is critical.

Made in Photoshop.

Michael Gvozdek 2024
